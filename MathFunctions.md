# Supported Mathematical Functions #
Here `t` is the taylor parameter.

  * `pow(t,x)` for x integer or real number. The integer version is analytical at 0.
  * `exp(t)`
  * `log(t)`
  * `sqrt(t)`
  * `erf(t)`
  * `sin(t)`
  * `cos(t)`
  * `asinh(t)`
  * `atan(t)`
  * `sinc(t)` Analytical at 0
